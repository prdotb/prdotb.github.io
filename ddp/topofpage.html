About this hotel

Stylish lobby with breathtaking views
Friendly and attentive staff
Home to the biggest saltwater pool in a European hotel!

If you fancy diving into five-star luxury with the Atlantic Ocean at your feet and Mount Teide as your backdrop, then look no further than Gran Melia Palacio de Isora. Lay down a towel at the sandy beachfront, cool off in the lagoon pool (you can't miss it!), or treat yourself to a massage at the Spa by Clarins (you're on your holidays after all). Plus, with a first-class mini club, an outdoor playground and a range of activities, there's plenty to keep the kids occupied, too.
